Welcome to Farkas 2.0


Instructions:

Adjust range by dragging up and down on the two notes on the main menu.

BPM and Game Duration can also be adjusted on the main menu.

To select your exercise and begin press Start Exercise.


Settings:

Change Instrument and Transposition.

Select Clefs

Choose desired Accidentals, including Double Sharps/Flats. This will affect the difficulty multiplier.

Reset High Scores.

Set Concert A (440Hz by default).

Set Tuning Sensitivity (higher value requires more precision). This will also affect difficulty multiplier.

Turn On/Off Feedback Comments (Too High/Low, Sharp/Flat etc).

Turn On/Off Screen Shake.

Adjust Noise Reduction (Set higher to prevent interruptions in noisier environments).


Modes:

Standard: Manually set duration and tempo.

Challenge: 2 Minutes at 72 BPM. Try to improve your high score by increasing range and adding more accidentals.

Blitz: Experts only! Starts slow and easy, see how far you can go.

Zen: No microphone feedback. Just random notes.

Fixed: Play the note shown to change the note. Designed for beginners.

Long Note: Time how long you can hold your selected note.
